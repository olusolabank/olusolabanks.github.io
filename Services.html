Solar micro and mini-grid project developers and operators

Millions of households all over the world are not connected to their national electricity grid and therefore lack sufficient energy to boost the local economy. Some communities have the electricity grid extended to them, but the local electricity companies do not have sufficient power to supply to these communities. Cephalead Energy will stand as a platform to bridge this energy gap. Cephalead Energy will continue to develop renewable energy mini grid facilities to meet the energy needs of these communities. Technology to be deployed will include the solar photovoltaic systems combines with other renewable energy system.

Installation and maintenance of solar stand-alone systems

Cephalead Energy will also extend services to individuals and corporate bodies who will want to generate their electricity using any of the renewable energy sources. The Company will greatly engage in the installation and maintenance of stand-alone solar photovoltaic systems.

Conducting renewable energy and energy efficiency studies

Research and development (R&D) have played pivotal role in the development of many economies in the world. The growth and pace of development in the renewable energy and energy efficiency subsectors to a large extent will depend on the quality of research conducted in these sectors. Cephalead Energy will provide services that will help to bridge the research gaps in the renewable energy and energy efficiency sectors especially in developing countries.
